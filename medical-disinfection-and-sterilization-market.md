<p><p>Medical disinfection and sterilization are crucial processes in healthcare settings to prevent the spread of infections. Steam sterilizers use high-pressure steam to eliminate microbes on medical instruments and equipment, making them safe for reuse. Ethylene oxide sterilizers use a gas to kill bacteria, viruses, and fungi on heat-sensitive instruments that cannot withstand high heat. Both types of sterilization play a vital role in maintaining a clean and safe healthcare environment, ultimately reducing the risk of healthcare-associated infections.</p></p>
