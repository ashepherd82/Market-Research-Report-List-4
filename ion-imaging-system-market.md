<p><strong>The Ion Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Velocity Map Imaging</li><li>2D Slice Imaging</li><li>3D Ion Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ion imaging systems are used in scientific research to map the velocity distribution of ions in a sample. Velocity Map Imaging provides a comprehensive view of ion velocities in a 2D format. 2D Slice Imaging captures a single plane of ion velocity information. 3D Ion Imaging constructs a full 3D representation of ion velocities within a sample. Each type of imaging system offers unique insights into the behavior of ions, allowing researchers to study chemical reactions and dynamics with precision and accuracy.</p></p>
