<p><p>Whey market is divided into four main types based on the different production processes and characteristics. Acid Whey is obtained during the production of acid-set cheeses, while Sweet Whey is derived from rennet-coagulated cheeses. Demineralized Whey has had minerals removed, making it suitable for infant formula and sports nutrition. Non-Demineralized Whey retains its natural mineral content and is used in various food products. Each type of whey caters to different industries and consumer needs.</p></p>
