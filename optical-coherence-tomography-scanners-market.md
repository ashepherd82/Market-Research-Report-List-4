<p><strong>The Optical Coherence Tomography Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spectral Domain (SD) OCT</li><li>Swept Source (SS) OCT</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) scanners are classified into three main types in the market. Spectral Domain (SD) OCT utilizes a broadband light source and a spectrometer to determine depth information. Swept Source (SS) OCT uses a tunable laser as the light source to achieve faster imaging speeds and deeper tissue penetration. Other types of OCT scanners can include full-field OCT, polarization-sensitive OCT, and enhanced-depth imaging OCT, each offering unique advantages and applications in various medical fields.</p></p>
