<p><p>Photomask for Semiconductor Market Types consist of EUV Mask, UV Mask, and DUV Mask. EUV Mask, or Extreme Ultraviolet Mask, is used in the most advanced semiconductor manufacturing processes. UV Masks are used in older semiconductor technologies, utilizing Ultraviolet light for patterning. DUV Masks, or Deep Ultraviolet Masks, are used for finer patterning in more modern semiconductor processes. Each type of mask has its own advantages and is selected based on the specific requirements of the semiconductor manufacturing process.</p></p>
