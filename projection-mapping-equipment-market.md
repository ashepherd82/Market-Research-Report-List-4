<p><p>Projection mapping equipment market can be classified into two main types based on technology- DLP (Digital Light Processing) and LCD (Liquid Crystal Display). DLP projectors use micro mirrors to reflect light and project images, providing high contrast and sharp image quality. On the other hand, LCD projectors use liquid crystal displays to create images, offering bright colors and good color saturation. Both types have their own advantages and are widely used in various applications such as entertainment, advertising, and event production.</p></p>
