<p><p>Cereal coatings come in two main market types: liquid and powder. Liquid coatings are typically applied to cereal products using spraying or enrobing techniques, providing a glossy finish and enhancing flavor. Powder coatings, on the other hand, are typically applied by dusting or tumbling the cereal in a dry powder mix, providing a matte finish and adding texture. Both types of coatings serve to enhance the overall taste, appearance, and shelf life of cereal products, catering to different consumer preferences and product requirements.</p></p>
