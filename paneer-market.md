<p><p>Paneer market is segmented into four main types based on the level of firmness: Soft Paneer, Semi-soft Paneer, Medium-hard Paneer, and Hard Paneer. Soft Paneer has a smooth texture and is commonly used in dishes like paneer tikka. Semi-soft Paneer is slightly firmer and more suitable for dishes like paneer bhurji. Medium-hard Paneer is firmer and holds its shape well in curries and stir-fries. Hard Paneer is the firmest variety and is ideal for grilling or frying. Each type caters to different culinary needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
