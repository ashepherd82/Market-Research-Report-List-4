<p><p>Dental examination lamps come in two main types - LED and Halogen. LED lamps are known for their energy efficiency, cool lighting, and long lifespan. They provide bright, white light that helps in accurate dental procedures. On the other hand, Halogen lamps are known for their warm, yellow light and are cost-effective. They are often used in dental clinics where color accuracy is not as critical. Both types of lamps play a crucial role in ensuring good visibility and successful dental treatments.</p></p>
