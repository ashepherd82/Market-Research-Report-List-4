<p><p>Clamp meters can be divided into three main types based on their technology: Current Transformer Clamp Meters, Hall Effect Clamp Meters, and Flexible Clamp Meters. Current Transformer Clamp Meters use a transformer to measure current, Hall Effect Clamp Meters utilize the Hall Effect to measure current, and Flexible Clamp Meters have a flexible coil that can be wrapped around irregularly shaped conductors. Each type of clamp meter offers different advantages and is suited for specific applications in the electrical industry.</p></p>
