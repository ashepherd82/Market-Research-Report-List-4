<p><strong>The Catalyst Coated MEA Membrane Electrode Assemblies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3-layer MEA</li><li>5-layer MEA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Catalyst Coated MEA Membrane Electrode Assemblies are commonly available in two types: 3-layer MEA and 5-layer MEA. 3-layer MEA consists of an anode and cathode layer sandwiching a proton exchange membrane, while 5-layer MEA includes additional catalyst layers on both sides of the membrane. The 3-layer MEA is widely used for fuel cell applications due to its simplicity and cost-effectiveness, while the 5-layer MEA offers higher performance and durability, making it suitable for more demanding applications.</p></p>
