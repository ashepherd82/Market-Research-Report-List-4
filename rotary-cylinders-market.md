They are available in three main types: pneumatic rotary cylinders powered by compressed air, hydraulic rotary cylinders utilizing pressurized hydraulic fluid, and electric rotary cylinders powered by electricity. Each type has its own set of advantages and applications, with pneumatic cylinders being cost-effective and suitable for high-speed applications, hydraulic cylinders offering high force capabilities, and electric cylinders providing precise control and programmability.</p></p>
