<p><strong>The Sweetening Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-intensity Sweetening Agent</li><li>Low-intensity Sweetening Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sweetening agents can be categorized into two main types: high-intensity and low-intensity sweeteners. High-intensity sweetening agents are several times sweeter than sugar and are used in small quantities to sweeten products without adding excess calories. Examples include aspartame, sucralose, and saccharin. On the other hand, low-intensity sweeteners provide sweetness with fewer calories than sugar and include options like stevia and erythritol. Both types of sweetening agents cater to different consumer preferences and dietary needs in the market.</p></p>
