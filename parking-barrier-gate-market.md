<p><ul><li>Straight</li><li>Crank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Parking barrier gate market types include straight and crank barriers. Straight barriers are typically operated by a motor that lifts the gate vertically, providing a simple and efficient way to control vehicle access. Crank barriers, on the other hand, use a manual hand crank to raise and lower the gate. This type of barrier is often used in situations where power supply is limited or unreliable. Both types offer reliable access control for parking lots and garages.</p></p>
