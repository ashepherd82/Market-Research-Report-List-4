<p><strong>The Security CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FSI</li><li>BSI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Security CMOS image sensors play a crucial role in ensuring safety and surveillance across various industries. The two main types of security CMOS image sensors are Front-Side Illuminated (FSI) and Back-Side Illuminated (BSI). FSI sensors have the light-sensitive elements positioned on the front side of the chip, while BSI sensors have them on the back side. BSI sensors generally offer better light sensitivity and lower noise levels compared to FSI sensors, making them ideal for low-light security applications.</p></p>
