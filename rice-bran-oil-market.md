<p><strong>The Rice Bran Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extraction</li><li>Squeezing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rice bran oil is produced through two main processes in the market - extraction and squeezing. In the extraction market, the oil is obtained by using solvents to separate the oil from the bran. This method is efficient but may lead to a loss of nutrients. On the other hand, in the squeezing market, the oil is extracted by applying pressure to the rice bran. This method is natural and retains more nutrients, but it is less efficient compared to extraction.</p></p>
