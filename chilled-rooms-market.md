<p><p>Chilled rooms market can be divided into three main types: walk-in cooler rooms, walk-in freezer rooms, and walk-in hybrid rooms. Walk-in cooler rooms are designed for storing perishable goods at a controlled temperature above freezing, while walk-in freezer rooms maintain temperatures below freezing to preserve frozen goods. Walk-in hybrid rooms combine the features of both cooler and freezer rooms, allowing for flexible storage options. These different types of chilled rooms cater to the specific needs of businesses in various industries such as food service, hospitality, and healthcare.</p></p>
