<p><p>UV LED for sterilization market includes three main types - UV-A LED, UV-B LED, and UV-C LED. UV-A LEDs emit long-wave ultraviolet radiation with a wavelength range of 315-400 nm, used for curing and tanning. UV-B LEDs emit medium-wave ultraviolet radiation with a wavelength range of 280-315 nm, used for medical and cosmetic applications. UV-C LEDs emit short-wave ultraviolet radiation with a wavelength range of 100-280 nm, highly effective for disinfection and sterilization purposes. Each type has specific applications and benefits in the UV LED market.</p></p>
