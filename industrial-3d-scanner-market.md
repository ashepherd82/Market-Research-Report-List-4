<p><p>Industrial 3D Scanner Market includes two main types: Stationary 3D Scanners and Portable 3D Scanners. Stationary 3D Scanners are fixed in one location and are ideal for scanning large objects or environments. They are often used in industries like automotive, aerospace, and construction. On the other hand, Portable 3D Scanners are handheld devices that can be easily carried to different locations for scanning small to medium-sized objects with precision. They are commonly used for quality control, reverse engineering, and prototyping in various industries.</p></p>
