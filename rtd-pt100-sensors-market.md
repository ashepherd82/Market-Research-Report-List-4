<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The RTD PT100 Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire-Wound RTD</li><li>Thin Film RTD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RTD PT100 sensors are commonly available in two main types: Wire-Wound RTD and Thin Film RTD. </p><p>Wire-Wound RTD sensors consist of a wire coil wound around a ceramic or glass core, providing high accuracy and stability. These sensors are typically more robust and can withstand harsh environments.</p><p>Thin Film RTD sensors, on the other hand, are made by depositing a thin layer of platinum on a substrate. They are known for their fast response times and compact size, making them suitable for applications where space is limited.</p></p>
