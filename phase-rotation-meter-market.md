<p><p>Phase rotation meters are used to determine the direction of rotation of three-phase systems in electrical installations. There are two main types of phase rotation meters - rotating and static. Rotating phase rotation meters use mechanical indicators to display the rotation direction, while static phase rotation meters use electronic displays. Rotating phase rotation meters are lightweight and easy to use, while static phase rotation meters provide accurate digital readings. Both types are essential tools for ensuring proper alignment and functionality of three-phase electrical systems.</p></p>
