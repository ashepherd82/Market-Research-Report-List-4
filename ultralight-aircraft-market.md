<p><p>The ultralight aircraft market can be broadly classified into three types: fixed wing, flex wing, and rotary wing. Fixed wing ultralight aircraft have rigid wings and are similar in design to traditional airplanes. Flex wing ultralights have a flexible wing structure and are also known as weight-shift control aircraft. Rotary wing ultralights, on the other hand, have rotating wings that provide lift and propulsion. Each market type offers unique characteristics and capabilities, catering to different preferences and flying experiences.</p></p>
